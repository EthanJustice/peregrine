About

Peregrine is a minimal, simple, and easy-to-use text editor. Instead of having a complicated user interface with hundreds of buttons (of which many are never used), Peregrine provides a distraction-free environment in order to help you get the most out of your writing sessions. Get Started Already!

Features

  • Four Themes
  • Charcter and Word Count
  • Minimal Settings and Customization
  • Text is Downloadable
  • Clean, sleek interface
  • Auto-saving (every 20 seconds) and manual saving capabilities
  • Saves workspace in the browser to recover work in case of loss
  • Over 20 Fonts and Sizes to Choose From
